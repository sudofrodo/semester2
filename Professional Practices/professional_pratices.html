CLO-1 1 Examine the different theories of ethical decision making. Understanding 7-9 CLO-2 2-3 Discuss intellectual property rights for open source software. Understanding 7-9 CLO-3 4-5 Investigate the impact of technological solutions to privacy issues in the changing world. Analyzing 2,7-9 CLO-4 1-9 Identify ethical issues by examining various case studies from ICT. Analyzing 2,7-9
